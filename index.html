Who we are

At Kolar Web Development, we are passionate about creating simple yet effective websites to help businesses grow online. Founded by a high school student with a deep interest in web development and technology, Kolar Web Development aims to provide affordable and quality websites tailored to the needs of small businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals.

With a foundation in HTML, CSS, and a growing knowledge of JavaScript and MySQL, we’re continuously learning and evolving our skills to stay at the forefront of the web development field. This website was created not only to showcase these skills but also to help expand our expertise and gain real-world experience in the field of web development.

Our mission is to offer professional web development services while continually improving and learning new technologies. By starting small, we aim to build a strong foundation for a full-fledged business in the future. Join us on our journey as we grow, learn, and help you succeed online.
